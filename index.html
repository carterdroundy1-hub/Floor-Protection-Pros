Floor Protection Pros

Premium floor protection for luxury custom homes.

Floor Protection Pros helps builders and homeowners protect hardwood, tile, and finished flooring surfaces during the construction process. In high-end homes, small damage can get expensive fast. The goal is simple: protect the finish, reduce repairs, and keep the project looking sharp the whole way through.

Learn More

Why This Business Matters

In luxury home construction, floors can get scratched, chipped, cracked, or stained after they are already installed. That creates rework, delays, and extra cost. Floor Protection Pros focuses on preventing those problems before they happen by installing professional-grade temporary protection materials and maintaining them throughout the build as needed.

This business is especially valuable on custom homes where finishes are expensive and expectations are high. Protecting the floor is a lot cheaper than replacing it.

        What Best-Practice Protection Includes

        Our best-practice model is built around layered protection, not a one-material shortcut. We combine Floorotex, cardboard, and multiple tape types to protect finished floors while keeping installs clean, secure, and practical for active job sites.

        We are constantly workshopping and improving the system to create stronger protection and a cleaner final presentation for builders and homeowners.

        Primary Surface Layer

        Floorotex

        Floorotex provides durable top-layer coverage around borders for a tight seal and helps create a cleaner, more professional install on premium homes.

        Supplemental Coverage

        Cardboard

        Cardboard is used where extra reinforcement, stair detailing, or tighter custom coverage is needed to keep vulnerable areas protected.

        Secure Installation

        Three-Tape Strategy

        Our installs use a combination of duct tape, Ram Board tape, and non-adhesive edge tape so protection stays secure without treating every surface the same way.

        Why Builders Pay for Floor Protection

        Good floor protection helps avoid expensive repairs on premium materials. A scratch on hardwood or chipped tile in a custom home can cost a lot more than the original protection service. That makes this a preventative service instead of just another cost.

        It also helps keep jobs cleaner, more organized, and more professional when clients walk through the home.
